FAQ

Fine print, up front

How and when do I get paid?

Monthly, in USDT, to the wallet on your account. Minimum payout $50 — smaller balances roll forward.

Who can become a partner?

Anyone with an audience of traders. No follower minimums, no approval queue.

How long does tracking last?

30 days from the last click — any account size they buy in that window counts as yours.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals and coupon-site postings are excluded and can end the partnership.

What are points actually for?

Nice try. Redemptions will be announced — everything earned before then counts in full. Summit partners hear first.

Do points expire?

Never. Your balance carries forward until redemptions go live — and after.
